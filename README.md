# Global-Market-Competitor-Analysis-Project
Global Market &amp; Competitive Analysis of Retail Performance using SQL &amp; Power BI

📊 Global Market & Competitive Analysis of Retail Performance

📌 Project Summary

This project provides a comprehensive analysis of global retail data to uncover market dominance, growth opportunities, pricing strategies, and product performance across countries.

The objective is to simulate a real-world competitive market analysis, enabling data-driven business decisions on expansion, pricing, and product strategy.



🎯 Business Problem

In a highly competitive retail environment, businesses need to answer:

* Which markets dominate revenue?
* Where are the best expansion opportunities?
* How do pricing strategies differ across regions?
* What products drive performance in each market?

This project answers these questions using data analysis and visualization.



🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* SQL Server (SSMS) – Data cleaning, transformation, and analysis
* Power BI – Interactive dashboards and business intelligence

📁 Dataset

The dataset used for this project can be accessed here:

🔗 https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/carrie1/ecommerce-data

Note: Due to GitHub file size limitations, the dataset is not included in this repository.

🧹 Data Preparation

* Combined multi-year datasets (2009–2011)
* Standardized data types (dates, numeric fields)
* Created calculated field:
   `Revenue = Quantity × Price`
* Removed null and inconsistent records
* Excluded non-product entries (e.g., POSTAGE, MANUAL)
* Cleaned dataset for accurate business insights



📊 Key Metrics

* Total Revenue: 17.7M+
* Total Customers: 5,800+
* Total Orders: 36,000+



🌍 Key Insights

🥇 Dominant Market — United Kingdom

* Contributes 83% of total revenue
* Highest customer base and transaction volume
* Operates on a high-volume, lower-price strategy



📈 Growth Market — Spain

* Strong customer activity
* Lower revenue compared to major markets
* Opportunity to improve monetization through pricing or product mix



💎 Premium Market — EIRE

* Highest average pricing
* Lower customer volume
* Indicates strong purchasing power and premium positioning



⚖️ Strategic Markets — Germany & France

* Stable customer base and revenue
* Scalable markets with strong growth potential
* Require optimized pricing and localized product strategies



📦 Product Insights

* Top Global Product: Regency Cake Stand 3 Tier
* France: Rabbit Night Light
* Germany: Regency Cake Stand 3 Tier

➡️ Insight:

* Mix of global product dominance and localized preferences



💰 Pricing Strategy Insights

* Significant variation across countries
* High-price markets (EIRE, Singapore) vs volume-driven markets (UK)
* Pricing influenced by customer behavior and transaction volume
